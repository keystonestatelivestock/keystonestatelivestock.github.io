About

About Us

Keystone State Livestock is a market-based livestock operation located in Southwestern Pennsylvania - a region with a long tradition of raising sheep on steep, hilly, pastures. We are carrying that legacy.

Grazing

Grazing

Intensive grazing

Our grazing program maintains forage at optimal condition, supporting excellent animal performance and carcass development. This practice virtually eliminates the use of medication in our lambs and benefits our local ecology.

Locally born and raised

Our ewe flock lives in Southwestern Pennsylvania, meaning all of our lambs are born and raised in Southwestern Pennsylvania. We never buy lambs from the Western US.
